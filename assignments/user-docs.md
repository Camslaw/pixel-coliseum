# Pixel Coliseum: Official Player Guide

Welcome to Pixel Coliseum! This guide will walk you through setting up your account, grouping up with friends, and surviving the arena. 

## 1. Getting Started: Account Creation
Before you can step into the Coliseum, you need to create your gladiator profile.

* **Sign Up:** Enter your email address to begin the registration process.
* **Verify Email:** Check your inbox for a verification link to confirm your account.
* **Create Profile:** Once verified, set your **Username** and create a secure **Password**. 
* **Log In:** Use your new credentials to sign in and access the Main Menu.

## 2. The Main Menu: Hosting and Joining
From the Main Menu, you can choose to create your own game or join an existing one.

* **To Host a Lobby:** Simply click the **Host** button. This will instantly generate a new lobby where you are the leader.
* **To Join a Lobby:** Obtain a Room ID from your friend. Paste or type it into the **Room ID** text box, then click the **Join** button to enter their lobby.

## 3. The Lobby: Preparation Phase
Once you are in a lobby, it's time to prepare for battle. Here is what you can do before the match starts:

* **Invite Friends:** Click to copy the **Room ID** displayed on the screen and send it to your friends so they can join you.
* **Choose Your Class:** Select your playstyle by clicking one of the class icons:
    * **Sword:** For close-quarters melee combat.
    * **Bow:** For ranged physical attacks.
    * **Magic:** For casting powerful spells.
* **Start Game:** If you are the host, click **Start Game** when everyone is ready.
* **Leave Lobby:** Click **Leave Lobby** if you need to return to the Main Menu.

## 4. Controls
The controls in Pixel Coliseum are simple and quick to learn:

* **W, A, S, D:** Move your character (Up, Left, Down, Right).
* **Spacebar:** Attack using your chosen class's weapon.

## 5. Gameplay & Objective
The Coliseum is unforgiving. Your goal is simple: **survive as long as you can.**

* **Round Progression:** The game is played in rounds. Once all enemies in a round are defeated, the next round begins. 
* **Scaling Difficulty:** With every new round, enemies will spawn in greater numbers and become increasingly difficult to defeat.
* **Power-ups:** Keep an eye out for random power-ups that will spawn across the map. Pick these up to gain temporary advantages in battle!
* **Game Over:** You play until your health runs out. Coordinate with your team, dodge enemy attacks, and try to beat your highest round survived!
